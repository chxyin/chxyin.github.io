Review

"Hide and Seek Alone" is a haunting and introspective song that showcases Tuyu's ability to create music that is both emotionally charged and musically intricate. The song is part of their 2023 album "Under Mentality" and features Rei's soulful vocals, Pusu's intricate guitar work, and Miro's delicate piano melodies.

The song's opening lines set the tone for the rest of the song: "I'm playing hide and seek all alone / In this maze of memories that I call home." These lyrics evoke a sense of isolation and introspection, as the song's lyrics explore the theme of loneliness and the struggle to find one's place in the world.

Rei's vocals on "Hide and Seek Alone" are haunting and emotional, conveying a sense of vulnerability and longing. Pusu's guitar work is intricate and complex, providing a musical counterpart to the song's introspective lyrics. Miro's piano melodies add a delicate touch to the song's sound, creating a sense of intimacy and introspection.

Overall, "Hide and Seek Alone" is a standout track on Tuyu's "Under Mentality" album and a testament to the band's ability to create music that resonates with listeners on a deep emotional level. The song's haunting sound and introspective lyrics make it a must-listen for fans of Japanese music and those who appreciate music that explores the complexities of the human experience.
