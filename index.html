Miro, the pianist, is a highly skilled musician with a background in classical music. He has a unique style of playing that adds depth and complexity to the band's music. His playing is often described as emotive and powerful, and his contribution to Tuyu's sound is invaluable.

They write songs about love, loss, and everything in between, and their lyrics often touch on themes of self-discovery and personal growth.
